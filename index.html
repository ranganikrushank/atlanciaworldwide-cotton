About

Cotton is a natural, breathable fabric known for its unmatched softness, durability, and versatility. It is hypoallergenic and eco-friendly, making it a perfect choice for clothing, home textiles, and industrial applications. With its ability to absorb moisture and regulate temperature, cotton provides comfort in all seasons.
